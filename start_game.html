1. I start out tall, but the longer I stand, the shorter I grow. Who am I ?






2. I have two hands, a face, always work, but stay in a place. Who am I ?






3. I have 13 hearts, but have no organs. Who am I ?






4. I fall, but I never break. I break, but I never fall. Who am I ?






5. I speak without a mouth, I hear without ears, I have nobody, I come alive with wind. Who am I ?






6. You answer me, but I never ask you a question. Who am I?






7. I have cities, but no houses. I have mountains, but no trees. I have water, but no fish. Who am I?






8. Spelled forwards I am what you do every day, spelled backward I am something you hate. Who am I?






9. Lighter than a feather, there is nothing in me, but the strongest man cannot hold me for more than a few minutes. Who am I ?






10. If you drop me, I am sure to crack. If you smile at me, I will always smile back. Who am I ?






11. I have a neck but no head. Who am I ?






12. I occur once a minute, twice every Millennium, but never in a hundred years. Who am I ?






13. I come down but I never go up. Who am I ?






14. I get shareper the more you use me. Who am I ?






15. I come from a mine, get surrounded by wood, everyone uses me. Who am I ?
